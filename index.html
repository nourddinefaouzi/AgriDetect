Our Products

Agricultural Sensor Station

Filiz stands as an IoT Sensor Station designed to empower farmers by offering comprehensive insights into soil and weather conditions within their fields. By harnessing real-time data, Filiz facilitates precise decision-making, such as optimizing irrigation, mitigating the risk of diseases and frost, and enabling informed choices when it comes to pesticide applications.

Digital Pest Tracking Station

PestTrap is an innovative IoT-based pest-tracking station that leverages the power of machine learning to accurately identify and quantify pests in real-time. This cutting-edge technology offers a robust solution for enhancing crop yields and mitigating the financial repercussions associated with pest-induced crop damage.

Digital Soil Analysis Device

The SoilScanner is a cutting-edge digital soil analysis device, designed to deliver real-time insights into soil nutrient levels while precisely calculating the ideal fertilizer type and quantity required. Complemented by a user-friendly mobile app, this innovative tool goes a step further by generating personalized fertilization programs, guaranteeing the utmost soil vitality and maximizing crop yields.
